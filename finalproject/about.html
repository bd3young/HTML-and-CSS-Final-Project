About

Myself

Hello, my name is Beau DeYoung and I was born and raised in Traverse City Michigan. I personally love Traverse City and do not want to live anywhere else. The things I love about this area is the craft beer, the sand dunes, the beaches and some of the nicest people I have ever met. I do more than just play video games, I love to longboard, hang out with my friends and enjoy the beautiful summers we have here.
